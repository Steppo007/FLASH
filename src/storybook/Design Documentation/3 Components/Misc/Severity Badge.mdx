## SeverityBadge

### Overview

A **Severity Badge** is a visual indicator used to represent the severity or importance of a status, message, or event. It is commonly used in dashboards, notifications, and alerts to quickly convey the level of urgency or priority through iconography.

* * * * *

### When to Use It

-   To indicate the severity of an issue or alert with an icon.

-   To provide a quick visual cue for the importance of a message or status.

-   To categorize items based on their priority or urgency.

#### **Avoid using Severity Badges when:**

-   The information does not require a visual indication of severity.

-   The context is purely informational and does not involve urgency or priority.

-   The badge is used purely for decorative purposes without conveying meaningful information.

### Variants

-   **Positive**: Indicates a positive status or successful outcome.

-   **Negative**: Indicates a negative status or error.

-   **Warning**: Indicates a warning or caution.

-   **Info**: Indicates informational content.

* * * * *

### Behavior

-   **Normal:** The badge displays the severity level with appropriate color and shape.

-   **Subdued:** The badge displays the severity level with a more muted color and less prominent shape.

### Dos and Don'ts

#### **Dos:**

✔ Use severity badges to clearly indicate the level of urgency or importance.\
✔ Ensure badges are easily distinguishable by color and shape.\
✔ Provide tooltips or additional information for badges if needed.

#### **Don'ts:**

✘ Don't use severity badges for non-urgent or purely informational content.\
✘ Don't use severity badges purely for decoration without conveying meaningful information.
