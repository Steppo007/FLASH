## ProgressSpinner

### Overview

A **Progress Spinner** is a visual indicator that shows the progress of a task or process. It is commonly used to indicate loading, processing, or waiting states in an application. The spinner provides feedback to users that an action is being performed.

* * * * *

### When to Use It

-   To indicate that a task or process is in progress.

-   To provide feedback during loading or waiting periods.

-   To show that the application is working on a background task.

#### **Avoid using Progress Spinner when:**

-   The task or process is instantaneous and does not require feedback.

-   A more detailed progress indicator (like a progress bar) is needed to show specific progress.

### Variants

-   **Indeterminate Spinner**: Spins continuously to indicate an ongoing process without a specific end time.

* * * * *

### Behavior

-   **Default**: The spinner rotates continuously to indicate an ongoing process.

* * * * *

### Dos and Don'ts

#### **Dos:**

✔ Use progress spinners to provide feedback during loading or processing times.\
✔ Ensure the spinner is visible and easily recognizable.\
✔ Use appropriate sizes and colors to match the application's design and context.

#### **Don'ts:**

✘ ✘ Don't use spinners for tasks that complete instantly.\
✘ Don't rely solely on the spinner for feedback---provide additional context if needed.\
✘ Don't use spinners purely for decoration without indicating actual progress.
