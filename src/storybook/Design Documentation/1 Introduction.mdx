Welcome to the Krones.Digital Design System
-------------------------------------------

The **Krones.Digital Design System** is the foundation for creating intuitive, cohesive, and accessible digital experiences across the Krones ecosystem. By providing a unified language, reusable components, and comprehensive guidelines, the system empowers teams to collaborate effectively and deliver innovative solutions that embody the Krones brand.

* * * * *

### Why the Krones.Digital Design System?

This system is more than a toolkit---it's a framework for excellence:

-   **Consistency:** Ensures all interfaces look, feel, and function as part of a cohesive whole.

-   **Efficiency and Cost-Effectiveness:** Speeds up design and development with reusable assets, reduces duplication of effort and optimizes resources to save time and costs.

-   **Scalability:** A system that evolves alongside the needs of Krones.

* * * * *

### What's Inside?

#### Fundamentals

The core principles and tools that form the basis of all designs:

-   **Token System:** A collection of predefined, reusable design tokens.

-   **Icons:** A standardized library of icons designed for clarity, scalability and seamless integration into interfaces.

-   **Colors:** A carefully curated palette for clarity, consistency, and branding.

-   **Data Visualization:** Tools and guidance for clear, impactful data representation.

-   **Typography:** Standards for text styling to ensure readability and hierarchy.

-   **Elevations:** Guidelines for depth and layering in UI design.

-   **Grid & Breakpoints:** Frameworks for creating responsive, adaptable layouts.

-   **Spacings:** Rules for consistent spacing to maintain visual harmony.

-   **Wording:** Best practices for clear and effective language, including capitalized labels.

#### Components

A library of ready-to-use, well-documented UI components that ensure consistency and ease of implementation:

-   **Variants:** Predefined options to address diverse use cases.

-   **Live Previews:** Interactive demos to explore component functionality.

-   **Light and Dark Modes:** Optimized for both light and dark themes, tailored specifically for HMI and desktop interaces.

#### Patterns

Reusable solutions to common design and interaction challenges. Patterns ensure consistency, reduce redundancy, and improve the overall user experience by streamlining workflows and providing cohesive, user-friendly interfaces.

* * * * *

### Who's This For?

The Krones.Digital Design System is designed to support:

-   **Designers:** To create cohesive, user-centered designs that align with the Krones brand.

-   **Developers:** To build efficient, maintainable interfaces using our comprehensive component library and documentation.

-   **Product Teams:** To align design and development for faster, more consistent workflows.
