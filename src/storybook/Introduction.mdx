
# Krones.Digital Design system

Welcome to the Krones Digital Design System, a unified platform meticulously 
crafted to streamline the development of our digital products worldwide. Serving 
as the ultimate reference for all Krones digital product teams, this system 
encompasses a spectrum ranging from initial guides to fundamental Core Components, 
fostering swift, consistent, and high-quality design and development. The 
collaborative efforts of our dedicated Krones and Syskron UX Dept. Team ensure 
constant evolution, aligning with the dynamic requirements of our designers, 
developers, and valued customers across the global Krones digital landscape.
